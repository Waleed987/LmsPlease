# Privacy Policy for NustFlow

**Last Updated:** January 27, 2026

## 1. Data Storage
All authentication information is **stored locally** on your device using the Chrome Storage API (`chrome.storage.local`).
- Your passwords are **encrypted** using AES-GCM encryption before being saved to local storage.
- The encryption keys are generated and stored locally on your device.

## 2. User Control
You have full control over your data:
- You can **view** and **update** your stored credentials at any time via the Extension's popup interface.
- You can **permanently delete** all stored data by clicking the "Clear" button in the Extension or by uninstalling the Extension.

## 5. Contact
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact the developer via the GitHub repository issues page.
